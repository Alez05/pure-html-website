Bringing spaces to life with plants in our jam

We work with internal teams looking for plants for their office, restaurant, event or space, as well as property developers, architects & interior designers. Our Plant Design team will create a scheme to suit your requirements whether that be outdoor, indoor, real or artificial.

How it works

  1. Consultation

    Fill in the form or send a message to projects@leafenvy.co.uk and our team will be in touch to discuss your plant scheme requirements.

  2. Design

    We take a bespoke and design-led approach to each project and will source hiqh quality plants and pots that are best suited to your space. Light levels, room temperature and other factors will all be considered when tailoring your planting scheme.

  3. Install

    Our Projects team will deliver and install your scheme at a time that suits you. We'll pot, prep and prune your plants prior to arrival and ensure they are well misted and watered before we go. All our plant displays are potted up with best practice and longevity in mind.

  4. After care

    Professional aftercare is available on request and bespoke to each plant design project. Just let us know you'd like to discuss this option too and we will create a quote for you.
